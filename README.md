## About

---

My work spans:
- **Applied physics** ‚Äî optics, birefringence, coherence dynamics, non-equilibrium structuring  
- **Computation** ‚Äî reproducible simulation design, numerical stability, GPU acceleration where appropriate  
- **Control & constraints** ‚Äî bounded recursion, drift-collapse behavior, invariant scaffolds

I do not optimize for casual skim-readers. I optimize for **technical clarity, falsifiability, and coherence under scrutiny**.

## Research Stance (Hard Boundary)

---

## How I Work

---

- **Prototype-first, constraint-led** ‚Äî ideas are abundant; only systems that survive explicit constraints, bounds, and failure conditions are kept.
- **Failure-mode driven** ‚Äî noise, drift, instability, and collapse are treated as diagnostic signals, not defects to be hidden or smoothed away.
- **Rigor over aesthetics** ‚Äî unconventional structure or presentation is acceptable when it prevents category errors, misinterpretation, or false generalization.
- **Observer diversity by design** ‚Äî multiple technical perspectives are used to surface blind spots and stress assumptions, not to manufacture agreement.
- **Iteration with memory** ‚Äî systems are evolved deliberately, with provenance, invariants, and prior failures preserved rather than overwritten.
- **Bounded claims only** ‚Äî anything that cannot be constrained, measured, or falsified is excluded from the record, regardless of appeal.
